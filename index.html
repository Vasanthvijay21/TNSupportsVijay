    About This Platform

    Home → About

    "People Support for CM Vijay" is an independent citizen-created platform to celebrate Tamil Nadu's democratic milestone peacefully. This is entirely non-commercial and non-partisan beyond celebrating the democratic outcome.

    What Is This?

    This platform allows citizens to create and share celebratory support posters featuring their own photos alongside campaign-style graphics celebrating Tamil Nadu's election result. It is a tool for democratic expression and joy-sharing among citizens.

    Who Built This?

    This is an independent project built by a Tamil Nadu citizen using open web technologies. It has no commercial funding, no organizational backing, and no affiliation with any political party or government body.

    Technology

    Built entirely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No server. No backend. No database. All poster generation happens on your device using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your photos are never uploaded anywhere.

    Contact

    For questions or concerns about this platform, please use the Contact page.

    Legal Notice: This website is not affiliated with the Government of Tamil Nadu, Election Commission of India, TVK party, or any official authority. This is an independent citizen expression platform only.

    Privacy Policy

    Home → Privacy Policy

    Short version: We collect nothing. Your photo never leaves your device. We have no server, no database, no analytics, no cookies (beyond browser defaults).

    Data Collection

    This website collects zero personal data. There is no server-side component. No analytics. No tracking pixels. No cookies set by this site.

    Image Processing

    All image processing happens entirely within your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. When you upload a photo:

    • It is loaded into browser memory only
    • It is drawn onto an HTML Canvas element on your device
    • The resulting poster is generated on your device
    • The download is created on your device
    • At no point does your photo leave your browser

    Third Party Services

    This site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may collect limited usage data per their privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

    Local Storage

    This site does not use localStorage, sessionStorage, or cookies to store any personal data.

    Children

    This site is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
